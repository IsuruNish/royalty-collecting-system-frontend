What is OSCA?

Outstanding Song Creator’s Association is an entity dedicated to artistes whose creativity lies with that unique medium of aesthetics –‘Song’. OSCA strives to ensure professional integrity and credibility, and stability of its membership. However this should not be interpreted as a trade union or a welfare association. OSCA is a totally non-governmental organization that is not affiliated to any publicized or non-publicized political leaning, nor does to represent any particular political bias. It is registered as non-profit organization with Sri Lanka Institute of Company Registration. The formation of this body has been recognized as a fulfillment of a long-felt need and also it is the sole organization that covers this multi-faceted field of artistic creativity most extensively. OSCA hopes to carve out a niche for the Sinhala Song and its three critical components-lyrics, music composition and vocal rendering at international level.
